What we do

Education

There are a lot of students in our society who are very excellent in their studies but are unable to presume their aims and dreams just because of financial constraints. Students who come from financially backward families are most often stranded in the middle their paths. We help them to achieve their high aims and achievements.

Shelter

Each and every day we hear of old people thrown away from their homes by families when they are of no use to others. A time comes in a man’s life when he is unable to help others physically and financially. Many incidents have occurred in our surroundings where old people become homeless and destitute. OFAN wishes to take care of these people and give them shelter and loving care.

Medicine

When you fall ill and it becomes terminal illness or have to undergo major surgery financially backward people lose all hope for want of money for their treatment. We are aiming to make available finance for medicines and treatment.
